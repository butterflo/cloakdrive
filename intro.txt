A few years ago, some Banshee pilots have accidentally discovered that cloaking a ship makes hyperspace jumps much more efficient.
While this was against the protocol of making sure ships are uncloaked before the jump, Terran military leaders have quickly realized its military implications, and soon factories begun the mass production of Cloakdrive engines.
This gave the entire Terran fleet an absolute advantage, and in a matter of weeks it won the war.
But this technology was too powerful for mortals to wield, and Terran factions kept on murdering each other from all directions..
